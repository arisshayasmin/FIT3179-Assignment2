Beyond the Olympics History

The most awaited international sports festival is around the corner, and fans from every corner of the world are as enthusiastic as ever. As of 2022, 29 Summer Olympics and 22 Winter Olympics had taken place, for a total of 51 Olympic events since 1896.

Lets delve deeper into the history!

Total Number of Medals Won by Each Country in Summer and Winter Olympics

With 2959 medals in total (Summer and Winter Olympics combined), the United States of America remains the leading country worldwide in terms of medal count (1173 of them gold). Moreover, with 12,434 medals, Europe currently is the leading continent with the highest medal count to date. North and South America together have earned around 4000 medals, placing them as the second-place continent up to this point.
